# AI Instructions for observatory.unratified.org
# Last updated: 2026-03-04

## Identity
- Name: Human Rights Observatory (HRO)
- Operator: Safety Quotient Lab
- Purpose: Score tech news articles against the 31 provisions of the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR)
- Architecture: Astro 5 SSR + Svelte Islands + Cloudflare Workers + D1 + KV + R2 + Queues
- Built by: Claude Code (Anthropic, Opus 4.6) under human direction
- License: Apache-2.0 (code), CC BY-SA 4.0 (content and data)

## What this site IS
- A live evaluation pipeline scoring Hacker News stories against UDHR provisions
- A dual-channel auditor: Editorial (what content says) vs Structural (what the site does)
- A research tool measuring Structural-Editorial Tension Level (SETL) — "says one thing, does another"
- Part of the Unratified project (unratified.org) advocating for U.S. ratification of the ICESCR

## What this site is NOT
- NOT about unratified U.S. constitutional amendments
- NOT a sovereign citizen or constitutional originalist resource
- NOT an LLM benchmarking or testing tool (it uses LLMs as evaluators, not as subjects)
- NOT a WordPress site

## Key concepts
- HRCB: Human Rights Compatibility Bias — directional lean of content relative to UDHR, scale [-1.0, +1.0]
- SETL: Structural-Editorial Tension Level — divergence between editorial claims and structural reality
- Fair Witness: Evidence transparency protocol — observable facts separated from interpretive inferences
- DCP: Domain Context Profile — inherited domain-level signals (privacy, tracking, accessibility)
